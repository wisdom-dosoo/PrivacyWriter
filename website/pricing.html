Frequently Asked Questions

Can I switch plans later?

Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. Changes take effect immediately.

How does the free limit work?

Free users get 25 AI requests (grammar checks, rewrites, etc.) every 24 hours. The counter resets automatically.

Do I need an API key for Pro?

No. PrivacyWriter Pro uses built-in local AI models. However, if you prefer using Claude, you can optionally add your own API key in the settings.

What is "Local Processing"?

It means the AI runs directly on your computer inside Chrome. Your text is not sent to our servers or Google's cloud servers.
